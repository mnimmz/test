Cannabinoid Water App Design

The Problem

The client asked me to redesign their current design for their app.
They were not happy with the use of gradients and felt that the design did not reflect their brand. 

The Solution

The new design incorporated an aquatic feel to reflect the client's product.
I removed the gradients and instead incorporated their design colors and more iconography.
