Lynn University Redesign

The Problem

The original Lynn.edu website had too much white space and no divisions or elements to break up content.
As well, many of the design elements were outdated.

The Solution

I went with the mobile-first approach.
I added more left and right padding for mobile. I utilized more overlays, horizontal lines and background colors to
break up content. 

Tools Used

Sketch 3, and Adobe Photoshop

Design Process

Because I used elements that already existed on the current website, I decided not to sketch out any wireframes.
